Mod with enchantment-related changes for 1.21.1 made by GK1H.
<h1>Features</h1>
<h2>Tradeable Wind Burst & Swift Sneak</h2>
Librarian villagers can give wind burst and swift sneak via trading.
<h2>Enchantability Changes</h2>
Changes to enchantability of items, requires <a href="https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/universal-enchants">Universal Enchants</a>.
